Owala FreeSip

The Owala FreeSip is a bright, stainless steel water bottle perfect for all of your hydration needs. The FreeSip comes in a variety of colors and sizes, allowing one to choose the water bottle that best fits their lifestyle! The triple layer insulation and leak-proof lid prevent spillage, and the water bottle is designed with a lock that doubles as a sleek handle to carry the bottle. FreeSip Twist

The Owala FreeSip Twist perfectly complements its FreeSip sister. The FreeSip Twist is also available in multiple colors and two sizes, but the bottle’s sleek design is where it stands out. The FreeSip Twist fits nicely into a cup holder or pocket, as its thin, stainless steel design makes it easy to travel with. With triple insulation, a twist lid, and the ability to hold cold, hot, and carbonated beverages, this water bottle is perfect for your quick hydration needs. FreeSip Tumbler

The Owala FreeSip Tumbler embodies the perfect tumbler. This stainless steel bottle is designed with a comfortable, ergonomic grip, so it never becomes too much to carry. Although the tumbler only comes in one size (40 oz), it has a dedicated lock to keep all of your liquids in one place. Similar to its FreeSip sisters, this bottle comes with triple layer insulation, and is both dishwasher-safe and hand-washable. Click “More” to discover the ease of the FreeSip Tumbler!
